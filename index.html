Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Univa

How secure is my data with Univa?

We take security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security protocols and undergo regular security audits to ensure your data is always protected.

Can I integrate Univa with other tools we use?

Absolutely! Univa offers integrations with over 100 popular business tools including CRM systems, project management platforms, accounting software, and more. Our API also allows for custom integrations.

How long does it take to set up Univa?

Most businesses can get up and running with Univa in less than a day. Our intuitive setup wizard and onboarding process make it easy to configure the platform to your specific needs.

What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include email support with a 24-hour response time. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority phone support, dedicated account management, and onboarding assistance.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start your trial.
